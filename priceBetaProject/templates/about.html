About Us

PriceBeta is a free and easy to use platform that offers an unbiased service for users to compare price of products and shipping options of multiple merchants/retailers and in the process pressenting the users with price offers that best meets their interest. Sellers can promote new products by sending emails to the subscribers about it.

PriceBeta do not sell any products. The information we collect is always up to date and accurate.
